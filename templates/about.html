About Us

What is Global Long Island?

Global Long Island (LI) is an initiative to measure and enhance the international engagement of Lons Island. International engagement has a substantial and dramatic impact on all aspects of our economy. The level of international engagement involves two main activities:

  • Attracting Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)
  • Promoting Exports

FDI and Exporting

Both FDI and exporting are important internaional business activities: they lead to job creation, foster economic growth, and enhanve global competitibeness of the region. Hence attracting FDI and promoting exports is an acute regional need.

Our Mission

Integrating international engagement into a region's economic development strategy requires a clear understanding of the significance and magnitude of FDI and exports within the regional economies, as well as their potential to crontribute to the community and internationally engaged companies. Global LI works to evaluate the global competitiveness and international engagement of Long Island and to determinie the region's international engagement readiness, capability, and commitment.
